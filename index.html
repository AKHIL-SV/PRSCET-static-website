PLACEMENTS

PRSCET has a very good record for placing students after training them which is done through CGPC (Career Guidance and Placement cell). The CGPC orientation programs equip the students to face interviews,resume preparation etc.

RESEARCH

PRSCET is a center for online training program for faculty members development program conducted by IIT Bombay & Kharagpur under the Ekalavya scheme. Research grant is provided to students who are going research projects.

PROFESSIONAL BODIES

PRSCET has many professional bodies student’s chapter functioning within the campus to promote engineering and education. These professional bodies built up the students to learn new technologies and bring out new innovations.

  • OUR MISSION

PRS College of Engineering and Technology focuses on moulding brilliant young talents, enabling them to take up challenging assignments in the highly competitive global scenario. Our team of dedicated and caring faculty strive to widen the horizon of learning thereby achieving excellent results for every student. The new millennium has become multifaceted and challenging for engineers. The focus of our college is to develop a global perspective to cope up with the fast changing technology scenario since students today are competing in a rapidly changing world. Let me reiterate that we strive to reach out to the star of perfection through an earnest academic pursuit for excellence and our efforts blossom into “service” through “excellence”.

  • To provide a broader understanding of the multi-disciplinary nature of engineering practice.
  • To make the student realize the impact of engineering on society and to provide opportunities in undertaking project work individually and in team work.
  • To help inculcate inter-personal and transferable skills through teamwork and project management.
  • To impart advanced specialist knowledge and practical know-how in a student’s chosen branch of study.

Library

The Library provides access for students to vast databases, full text journals and an extensive collection of books. The fully computerized college central library is equipped with more than 14000 volumes of Technical books.

Lecture Halls

The college has an air conditioned seminar hall with state-of-the-art audio visual aids for technical presentations. Modern and well furnished class rooms provide a congenial atmosphere for learning.

Canteen

The college canteen adheres to the highest degree of quality, hygiene and provides students and faculty with tasty food at affordable rates.
